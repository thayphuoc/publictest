A. Informal Email - Describing a Crime/Event

1. Structure

Opening: "Hope you are well." / "Guess what happened..."

Body (Narrative): Set the scene -> The Incident -> Reaction.

Closing: "Write back soon." / "Best wishes,"

2. Key Vocabulary (Sequencing)

  • Start: At first... / While we were...
  • Middle: Suddenly, Just then, At once.
  • Time passes: A short while later, After a while.
  • End: In the end, Finally.

B. Formal Letter - Complaint/Report

1. Structure

Salutation: Dear Sir or Madam (Yours faithfully) / Dear Mr. X (Yours sincerely).

Para 1: Reason for writing ("I am writing to express my disappointment at...")

Para 2: Main Reason/Details ("My main reason for objecting is...")

Para 3: Concession ("I realize that... However...")

Para 4: Conclusion ("Unless... / All in all...")

2. Key Vocabulary (Linking)

  • Reason: Since, As, Because.
  • Condition: Unless (Tr·ª´ khi).
  • Preference: Rather than.
  • Contrast: However, Though.
